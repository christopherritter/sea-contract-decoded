LEAVE FOR PROFESSIONAL STUDY (ยง 14.13)

a. Professional leave without pay will be granted on the basis of one (1) full
semester or one (1) full year. Leave will be granted only for full-time graduate
study or completion of an undergraduate degree. Earned credits must be filed
with the Superintendent prior to reemployment in the Springboro Community City
School System. Upon request, an extension of one (1) full year of leave may be
granted.
