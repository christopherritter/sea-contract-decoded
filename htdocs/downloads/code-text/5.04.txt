                                     CONTRACT

BOARD MEETINGS (ยง 5.04)

a. Prior to each BOARD meeting, the President of the ASSOCIATION shall be
provided with an agenda and minutes of previous meetings as included with the
agenda. Any addendum to the agenda shall be distributed to the ASSOCIATION
President at the BOARD meeting.

b. The President of the ASSOCIATION shall have the right to speak at BOARD
meetings on any item of concern to members at a time designated by the BOARD.

c. The President of the ASSOCIATION shall have the right to speak at BOARD
meetings on any item of concern to members and at a time designed by the BOARD.
