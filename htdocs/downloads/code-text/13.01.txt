                                     CONTRACT

SCHOOL DISCIPLINE PROCEDURES (ยง 13.01)

a. The BOARD is responsible for adopting a code of student conduct.

b. The Principal of each building shall develop with his/her faculty and staff a
means of implementing the adopted code of a student conduct for his/her school
building that leads to appropriate student discipline.

c. Rules and regulations governing discipline and procedures for student control
shall be reviewed by the Building Principal periodically with the faculty.

d. A Building Code of Conduct regarding discipline shall be presented to each
employee at the beginning of each school year.
