                                     CONTRACT

RIGHT TO STRIKE (§ 2.10)

a. Nothing herein shall prohibit the ASSOCIATION from exercising its right under
Section 4117.14(D) of the Ohio Revised Code, provided the ASSOCIATION has given
the BOARD and the State Employee Relations Board ten (10) calendar days’ prior
notice, in writing, of its intent to strike on or after the expiration date of
this Agreement. Such written notice shall contain the day and time the strike
shall commence and shall otherwise comply with the applicable provisions of the
Ohio Revised Code.
