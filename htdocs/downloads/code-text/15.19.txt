                                     CONTRACT

IEP/504 MEETINGS (ยง 15.19)

a. A building principal may direct employees to attend any lEP meeting or 504
meeting that occurs outside the normal teacher working day. Employees who attend
five (5) IEP/504 meetings outside the normal teacher working day in one school
year will receive a payment of $100 in the last pay in June. Any employee who
attends more than five (5) IEP/504 meetings outside the normal working day will
receive a payment of $20 per meeting for each such meeting over five (5)
meetings in the last pay in June.
