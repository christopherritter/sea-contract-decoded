                                     CONTRACT

EMPLOYEE JOB DESCRIPTIONS (ยง 15.18)

a. The Superintendent has developed job descriptions for all current jobs which
have been provided to the ASSOCIATION. lt is the prerogative of the
Superintendent to develop new job descriptions for bargaining unit positions.
Upon development, the Superintendent shall forward copies of proposed job
descriptions for new positions and/or for positions which are being revised to
the ASSOCIATION President for input prior to formal creation or revision of said
positions.
