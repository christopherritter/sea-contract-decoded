                                     CONTRACT

DISCIPLINE (ยง 11.01)

a. The BOARD/Administration shall have the right to discipline; (e.g., verbal
warning, and/or disciplinary suspension without pay) for good and just cause.
Disciplinary action of any type taken by the BOARD/Administration shall be
subject to the grievance/arbitration provisions of this Agreement.

b. The procedures set forth in Sections 4.04 shall be applicable to the issuance
of discipline.
