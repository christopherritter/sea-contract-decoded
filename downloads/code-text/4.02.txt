                                     CONTRACT

DEFINITIONS (§ 4.02)

a. Grievance – A grievance is a complaint regarding an alleged violation,
misinterpretation or misapplication of any provision(s) of this Agreement;
provided:

1. If a specific section of this Contract limits the parameters and use of this
grievance procedure, such limitations shall be followed.

b. Appropriate Administrator – The Administrator at the lowest level who has
authority to consider the grievance.

c. Grievant – The “grievant” shall mean the employee(s) or the ASSOCIATION
filing the grievance.

d. Days – The term “days” shall mean calendar days.
