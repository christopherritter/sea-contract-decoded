                                     CONTRACT

MILEAGE REIMBURSEMENT (ยง 18.05)

a. An employee required by the administration to drive his/her personal vehicle
on School District business shall receive mileage reimbursement for such driving
at the IRS mileage reimbursement rate. All vehicle usage must be approved in
advance by the School administration and reimbursement must be requested on the
appropriate form and approved by the appropriate Building Administrator for
payment.
