                                     CONTRACT

LIMITED CONTRACT SEQUENCE (ยง 16.01)

a. An employee sewing under a limited contract shall be considered, at the
expiration of his/her present limited contract, for a multi-year limited
contract, providing the following qualifications have been met:

1. The employee has served at least three consecutive years under one-year
limited contracts.

2. The employee is recommended on the basis of evaluation for a multi-year
contract by the Superintendent.

3. Nothing herein shall preclude the BOARD from offering the employee a one-year
contract on the basis of evaluation.
