CHANGE IN LAW DURING AGREEMENT (ยง 2.12)

a. If, during the term of this AGREEMENT, there is a change in any state or
federal law which would invalidate any provision of this AGREEMENT. The parties
will meet to negotiate any necessary change in the Agreement relative to the
affected provision within thirty (30) calendar days upon request of either party
and in accordance with provisions outlined in this Article.
