                                     CONTRACT

ASSOCIATION RELATED MEETINGS (§ 5.08)

a. The local ASSOCIATION President and officially elected delegates or
alternates shall be given leave by the BOARD to attend the annual NEA/OEA
Representative Assemblies. The ASSOCIATION shall be responsible for paying the
cost of the substitute for said delegates.

b. The ASSOCIATION shall also be allowed to purchase up to five (5) days‘
release time for the President or any authorized designee by reimbursing the
District for the cost of the substitute.
