                                     CONTRACT

IRS SECTION 125 PLAN (§ 18.04)

a. The BOARD has in place a Section 125 Plan pursuant to which the employee
portion of any insurance benefits and employee-paid medical expenses and
employee-paid dependent care expenses may be paid with “before tax” dollars.
The maximum amount which may be placed in this account for medical expenses
during any calendar year shall be Two Thousand Dollars ($2,000.00). The maximum
amount dependent care expenses IS Five Thousand Dollars ($5,000,000).

b. The administrative cost of the Section 125 Plan shall be paid by the BOARD.
