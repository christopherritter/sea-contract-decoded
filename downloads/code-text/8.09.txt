a. The parties state that it is their express intention and agreement that the
evaluation procedures set forth in this Article are the sole evaluation
procedures applicable in the Springboro Community City School District for the
employees in the bargaining unit covered by this AGREEMENT and that such
evaluation procedures totally replace and are in lieu of evaluation procedures
set forth in the Ohio Revised Code Section 3319.111.
